Features

Everything a game booster should be

A native-speed engine with a modern interface - light on RAM, honest about what software can and cannot do.

One-click Game Boost

Suspends background apps, raises game priority, switches the power plan, pauses Windows Update & friends - and reverts every change when you're done.

Launcher-aware library

Latency & input tuning

Insights & analytics

Safety by design

How it works

Boost on. Play. Boost off. Nothing left behind.

Every change goes on an undo stack the instant it's made, so it can be restored to the exact value it had before - automatically when your game exits, when you press Undo, when the app closes, even if it crashes.

The honest part

FAQ

Questions, answered straight

Will this actually increase my FPS?

Often, yes - by freeing CPU/RAM from background apps, switching to a performance power plan, and reducing scheduling and latency overhead. How much depends entirely on your PC and what's running. We don't promise a magic number, because nobody honestly can.

Is it safe? Can it break Windows?

Every change is tracked and reverted automatically - on game exit, on Undo, on app close, and via a crash backstop. Only small, visible allowlists are ever touched, OS-critical processes are protected, and antivirus/security software is hard-blocked in code.

Will it get me banned by anti-cheat?

No injection is used. Games launch through their own launcher, and FPS comes from Intel PresentMon (Windows ETW) - the same approach professional overlays use. Nitro Forge never reads or writes game memory.

Why does it want Administrator?

Pausing Windows services, the network/scheduling latency tweaks, and PresentMon's ETW capture need elevation. The app runs fine without admin - those specific features are just skipped and clearly marked.

Is it really free?

Yes. No accounts, no ads, no paid tier, no telemetry you didn't opt into. The engine is open source.
