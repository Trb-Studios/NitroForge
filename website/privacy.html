Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

The short version

Nitro Forge runs entirely on your PC. It does not require an account, does not show ads, and sends nothing off your machine unless you explicitly configure and enable reporting.

Data stored locally

  • Settings, performance history (CPU/GPU/RAM/FPS samples), logs and cached game box art - all in %LOCALAPPDATA%\NitroForge.
  • Crash reports are written locally as JSON files you can open and read.

Data that can leave your PC (opt-in only)

  • Crash / bug / feedback reports - only if you paste a Discord webhook or website endpoint into Settings and enable sending. Reports contain: app version, OS version, CPU thread count, RAM size, the error stack trace, your typed feedback and (if enabled) recent app log lines.
  • Game box art - fetched from Steam's public CDN by game ID. This is a normal image download and includes no personal data.

What is never collected

  • No personal files, browsing history or keystrokes.
  • No account credentials for Steam/Epic/Riot or any launcher.
  • No advertising identifiers, no data sales, no third-party analytics by default.

Contact

Questions? Use the in-app feedback form (Settings) or the Support page.
